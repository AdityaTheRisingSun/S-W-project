About This Home

"DELHI RICH SOCITY"

An ode to elegance and design mastery, this exquisite residence by Nicholas Day, with landscaped gardens by Jack Merlo, redefines luxury, meticulously curated for those with an eye for the extraordinary. From its unparalleled level of detail to panoramic views of the Melbourne skyline, it captures timeless beauty and modern glamour, where each space speaks to an artful lifestyle of prestige and refinement.
A commanding reception hall with marble floors introduces the home's grandeur, flowing seamlessly into a refined office with a lavish ensuite and an inviting formal sitting room, each with a gas fireplace. Set with a full-height window wall, the palatial living and meals area with a striking gas fire to match is served by an extraordinary chef's kitchen appointed with a comprehensive suite of Gaggenau appliances, a fully-equipped butler's pantry and Arabescato Vagli marble countertops. Sublime landscaped private gardens embrace a resort-style setting within the northern pavilion, with an in-built outdoor kitchen/BBQ, seamless access for the host and a heated self-cleaning swimming pool. A solid marble staircase is a majestic prelude to the upper and lower floors, accompanied by a commercial-grade lift that accesses all levels.
